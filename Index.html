Learning from the Indian Cultures, which has a strong bend towards helping those in need, this initiative aims to help the less fortunate that find it difficult to secure food for their families after a day of strenuous labour. This project aims to act as a middle men, simply delivering the excess from the more fortunate to those in need, in a bid to raise their standard of living

Blueprint

Picture Standing in the middle of an ocean looking at the endless stretch of blue, you realise the majesty of the water body but you start wondering the significance of a single droplet that constitutes the thalassic. The tiny droplet seems so insignificant in comparison to the ocean that you start questioning its contribution to the ocean. Starting this project our team members felt like these tiny droplets-insignificant. Our scepticism stemmed from the belief that if major names in the industry can’t solve hunger what can four teenagers barely though their adolescence do about this global crisis. Hunger is a pressing issue which grips every nation of this world...

A Small Step to a Brighter Future

  • 1

  • 2

  • 3

  • The food that we produce today statistically has the capacity to feed 10 billion people (1.5 times the current population), yet millions sleep with an empty stomach every night. When you sign up with this initiative you will be provided with a vessel that you would keep with you for a month.

  • For the entirety of the month you will put aside a fistful of raw rice/pulses/grains (The ration you choose below) in the vessel provided, before preparing food for your own family. The completely filled vessel would be collected by one of our volunteers at the end of the month.

  • Your contribution to the program would help feed not only your family but your excess will help feed a family less unfortunate than yours. The ration collected would be cleaned, repackaged and distributed to charities which specialise in feeding those in need.
